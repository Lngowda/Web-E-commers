Hardware

Benefits of using E-Vyavahaar

Safe and Secure

The GST billing and accounting software has been designed with utmost care. All the business data entered in the software is 100% encrypted And stored anonymised to prevent hackers, malware, and virus attacks. We go the extra mile to protect your information from misuse.

Automatic Backup

All the data stored in the GST invoicing software is automatically backed up whenever the phone is connected to the internet. The backup data is stored in encrypted form and can only be accessed by the owners or assigned professionals. The billing app makes your data secure with regular back-ups, even if you lose or change your mobile phone..

E-Vyavahaar App

E-Vyavahaar app helps MNC companies to manage their business transactions in multiple languages. Currently, the desktop version of the software is available only in English, and the team is working on adding Hindi and Hinglish languages..

Offline Billing

The billing software not only works in online mode but is also available in offline mode with limited features. Users can create GST & non-GST bills using the software in offline mode, i.e. they can make & edit bills when there is no internet connection..

Whatsapp Support

The billing app has a separate ‘Help & Support’ function to help users with FAQs and video tutorials on ‘How to’ use the app features. If they don't solve the query, users can reach out to our customer support team anytime through Whatsapp or e-mail..

Easy Data Transfer

For users migrating from other billing softwares, the myBillBook application provides an easy migration option. Users can easily transfer their data from other billing applications like Vyapar, Tally, Marg, Busy, etc.

Item Library

E-Vyavahaar billing software with GST has a standard item library of features FMCG & Pharma items. You can quickly create your inventory list from this library and start creating professional bills from this billing app.

Send Business Card & Greetings

The GST desktop billing application provides users with an option to create professional business cards, & share them on WhatsApp with their customers/vendors. They can also share customised greetings with their business friends & family using this billing app & make them feel special.
